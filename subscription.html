Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. Premium subscriptions will automatically renew using your selected payment method unless canceled.

Can I cancel my Premium subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Premium subscription at any time. If you cancel during your billing period, you'll continue to have access to Premium features until the end of that period. We don't charge cancellation fees.

Is there a free trial for Premium?

We occasionally offer free trials for new users. Currently, we have a 7-day free trial available for first-time Premium subscribers. After the trial period, your subscription will automatically continue unless canceled.

How does the priority delivery work?

When you place an order as a Premium member, our system prioritizes your delivery ahead of non-Premium orders. Our drivers are notified of Premium deliveries and will route accordingly. We guarantee delivery within 15-20 minutes in urban areas, or your next delivery is free.

Are the discounts applicable to all fuel types?

Premium discounts apply to all standard fuel types (Regular, Premium, Diesel). Some specialty fuels or additives may be excluded from discounts. The discount percentage may vary based on current market conditions but will always be at least 15% for Premium members.
